## Audience Attendance and Growth
- Is there seasonality (winter slump, holiday spike, summer lull)? Compare attendance at different months of the year excluding months of 2025
- Are first-time visitor trends improving or declining over the past two years?
- Do months with high first-time visitors also have high total attendance?
- Are donations correlated with returning visitors?

## Event Performance
- Which events drew the highest vs lowest attendance and first time visitors?
- Does event duration correlate with total attendance?
- How does average ticket price vary across events?
